Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Water Cycle?

The water cycle is the continuous movement of water within the Earth and atmosphere. It involves processes like evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and collection.

Is this learning website free to use?

Yes! All the modules, animations, and quizzes are completely free for students to access.

Can I download or print the notes?

Currently, you can view all materials directly on this site. We are working on a downloadable PDF feature to be added in the future.

Who is this website designed for?

This site is designed primarily for secondary school students who want a simple, visual, and interactive way to master Science topics.
