Lessons Stage 4: Collection

🌊 Collection

The final stage that keeps the water cycle moving.

Phase: Water Storage

What is Collection?

Collection is the stage where water gathers in rivers, lakes, oceans, streams, and underground reservoirs.

From these massive water sources, the Sun will heat the water again, allowing it to turn back into vapour, and the cycle continues forever.

The Collection Journey

  • 1 Rainwater reaches Earth's surface.
  • 2 Water flows into rivers and lakes.
  • 3 Some soaks into the ground (groundwater).
  • 4 Most water reaches the oceans.
  • 5 Solar heat restarts evaporation.

Did You Know?

About 97% of the Earth's water is stored in our oceans. They are the largest collection point in the water cycle!
