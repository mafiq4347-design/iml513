Lessons Stage 1: Evaporation

☀️ Evaporation

The first major step in the continuous water cycle journey.

Phase: Liquid to Gas

What exactly happens during Evaporation?

Evaporation is the crucial process where **heat energy from the Sun** warms up the water sitting in our oceans, rivers, and lakes.

As the temperature rises, the liquid water gains enough energy to break its bonds and transform into an invisible gas called **water vapor**, which then rises upwards into the atmosphere.

Key Takeaways to Remember

Solar Heat Required

The entire process is powered entirely by thermal energy from the Sun.

State Change

Water undergoes physical transition changing from liquid into a gas form (vapor).

Surface Phenomenon

This transformation primarily happens on the top surface layer of large water bodies.
